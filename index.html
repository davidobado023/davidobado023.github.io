COMPANY BACKGROUND

Since 2019, RC EXPORT has established itself as a vital independent international trader, supplying and facilitating the needs of clients from all over the world in major areas like construction, agriculture, appliances, clothing, and medical supplies. We have the capacity to be a crucial trader on the global market because to our trustworthy partner suppliers and factories, solid operation and logistics team, and multinational marketing partners.

RC EXPORT attained this status as a result of the founder's and workers' joint efforts, dedication, and perseverance. The desire to achieve greatness is one trait shared by every person associated with RC EXPORT. This pursuit of excellence gives us the drive to offer our loyal customers outstanding quality, shorter lead times, and superb delivery.

MISSION

Our mission is to build relationships, not transactions. We will place our clients at the core of the business and provide the best experience for them, and deliver results. We will provide excellent quality service both our clients and employees.

VISION

We are committed to be the top of mind commodities supplier all over the globe. We will ensure excellent customer service for every client stepping in our premises. We assure our employees a friendly environment and workplace for their growth and success.
